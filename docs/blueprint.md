# **App Name**: EncomiendaYA

## Core Features:

- Store Account Creation: Allow admins to add new store accounts with specific details and credentials.
- Delivery Account Management: Enable creation and management of delivery personnel accounts, including assigning delivery zones.
- Admin Account Management: Secure admin account creation and permission settings to manage the platform.
- Storefront Index: Display available stores in a list or grid layout with key information like name and location.
- Item Management: Stores can manage their product listings by adding, updating, and removing items, complete with descriptions and pricing.
- Order Tracking: Customers can track their order status in real-time with updates provided by delivery personnel. The LLM will act as a tool to parse customer reviews and automatically tag drivers.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saturated orange (#FF7900) to evoke a sense of energy and speed, reflecting the delivery service.
- Background color: Light orange (#FFF2E6), a very low saturation variation of the primary, to provide a bright, clean backdrop.
- Accent color: Analogous yellow (#FFC600) to highlight calls to action and important information, drawing the user's eye.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a modern and readable sans-serif, to ensure a clean and accessible reading experience.
- Use consistent and recognizable icons to represent different store categories and actions within the app.
- Prioritize a grid-based layout for store listings to maintain visual balance and readability.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and animations to enhance user interaction, such as loading states and order confirmations.
